# [DCProof.net](https://www.dcproof.net)

![](/wp-content/portfolio/dcproof.png)

DCProof is an automated AI-powered quality-control and content-control service for short-form media: cinema, TV, out-of-home, radio and social media advertising (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok), and film trailers.

Upload a spot or trailer, and DCProof analyses every frame and every second of audio. Minutes later you get a clear, shareable report — no screening room required.

* [DCProof QC Report](https://www.dcproof.net/products/qc-report/) : Per input video, a full quality- and content-control report: a human-readable PDF and a JSON data blob, ready for a reviewer or for your own systems. Submit via the API or a simple submit-URL. 
* [DCProof Rating Tool](https://www.dcproof.net/products/rating-tool/) : A review web app for age rating: jump between the timecoded moments of interest in a spot, check them against your market's standard — FSK, BBFC, Kijkwijzer, GoedGezien and more — and record the decision.
